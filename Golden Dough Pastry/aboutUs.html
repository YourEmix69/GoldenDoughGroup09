About Us

  Shop History

Golden Dough | Pastry, a brand that was established since 1983 by Al-Yazidi Abdulaziz Hussein Ghaleb and his partner Emon Rifat Hasan. Al-Yazidi Abdulaziz, the owner of the shop, dedicated their passion towards bakery delicacies, the both decided to open their shop called Golden Dough | Pastry. During his journey, Golden Dough | Pastry was recognized by the local where the shop open which in Gombak,Selangor. With various of multiple of celebrities and well-known figure that comes to their doorstep, this bakery shop has served outstanding quality of pastries, sweets and also unique tastes that always deliver to everyone who comes to their shop.

  Mission

 Create a bakery which makes the best quality baked goods fresh daily.

 To provide supreme product and customer experience using quality ingredients and new techniques to create a product truly superior in taste and design.

 Create an atmosphere where employees can learn, grow, and innovate, while holding true to the roots of traditional baking.

  Vision

 To provide baked goods of exceptional quality to every household in Malaysia.
